[User Story]
As a parent, I want to register a tutor account on GrowGreen so that I can offer my teaching services to students.

[Business Rules]
- Email must be unique and follow a valid email format.
- Password must be at least 8 characters, contain letters and numbers.
- Phone number must be a valid Vietnamese phone number.
- All required fields (name, email, password, confirm password, phone) must be filled.

[Acceptance Criteria]
AC01: When user fills all required fields with valid data and clicks "Đăng ký", system creates a new tutor account and redirects to tutor dashboard.
AC02: When email is missing, show validation message under Email field.
AC03: When email is invalid format, show validation message about invalid email.
AC04: When password and confirm password do not match, show validation message about mismatched password.
AC05: When phone number is invalid, show validation message about wrong phone format.
